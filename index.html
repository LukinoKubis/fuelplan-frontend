How would you like to use Fuelplan?

Install on iPhone

1

Open in Safari

This must be done in Safari — not Chrome or Firefox. If you're in another browser, copy the URL and paste it into Safari.

2

Tap the Share button

At the bottom of Safari, tap the Share icon (the box with an arrow pointing up).

3

Tap "Add to Home Screen"

Scroll down in the share sheet and tap "Add to Home Screen". Confirm the name and tap Add.

4

Open from your home screen

Fuelplan will open full-screen like a native app — no browser bar, no distractions.

App installed! You're all set.

Install on Android

Your browser supports one-tap install!

1

Open in Chrome

For the best experience, open this page in Chrome on your Android device.

2

Tap the menu

Tap the three-dot menu ⋮ in the top-right corner of Chrome.

3

Tap "Add to Home screen"

Select "Add to Home screen" (or "Install app" if Chrome shows it). Confirm and tap Add.

4

Launch from home screen

Find the Fuelplan icon on your home screen and tap it — it opens like a native app.

You're offline — your plan is still available
